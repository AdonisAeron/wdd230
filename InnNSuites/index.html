Columbia SC Temple

This Temple in Columbia, SC was first announced on 11 September, 1998. The groundbreaking for this temple started on 5 December, 1998 with the temple being finished and dedicated on 16 October 1999. It is located at 2905 Trotter Rd Hopkins, SC.
